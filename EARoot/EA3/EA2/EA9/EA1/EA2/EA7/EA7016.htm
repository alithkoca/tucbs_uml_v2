The class SF_Process (Figure 13) is an instance of the «metaclass» GF_FeatureType (ISO 19109:2005), which therefore represents a feature type. SF_Process is abstract, and has no attributes, operations or associations. It serves as the base class for processes associated with the design and preparation of sampling features. The purpose of a sampling process is to generate or transform a sampling feature.  <br/><br/>

In many applications specimen preparation procedures are applied to the material prior to its use in an observation.
