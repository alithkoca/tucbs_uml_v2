The LC_GrowthFormCharacteristic component is a subtype of LC_LandCoverElementCharacteristic. The component LC_GrowthFormCharacteristic is related to the LC_VegetationElement metaobject by the relation <i>growthFormCharacteristics</i>. This same relationship, of course, also applies to the metaobject LC_GrowthForm which is a subtype of LC_VegatationElement. The component LC_GrowthFormCharacteristic may be used to refine the metaobject LC_VegetationElement or any of its subtypes to permit the generation of a more specific land cover feature class in an LCCS. <br/>LC_GrowthFormCharacteristic has nine subtypes LC_FloristicAspect, LC_AllometricMeasurement, LC_GrowthFormAge, LC_TreeAreaManagementPractise, LC_BurntStatus, LC_DeadStatus, LC_WaterStress, LC_GrowthFormIllness, and LC_VegetationArtificiality.<br/><br/>
