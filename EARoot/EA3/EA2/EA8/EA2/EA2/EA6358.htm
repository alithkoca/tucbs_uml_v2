The LC_LichenAndMoss metaobject is a subtype of LC_GrowthForm. It has two subtypes LC_Lichen and LC_Moss. This metaobject, when instantiated, allows the generation of a general land cover feature class in an LCCS for all vegetation covered surfaces where the cover is a herbaceous growth form. <br/><br/>
