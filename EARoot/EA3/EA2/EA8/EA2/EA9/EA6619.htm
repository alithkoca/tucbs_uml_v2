This example illustrates the "on Top" relationship. It describes boulders with moss on top of the boulder in a grassland. Three stratum are defined. The first stratum contains the herbaceous growth form corresponding to grassland. The second contain boulders. The third contains moss and includes an "onTop" relation to the stratum before it, the stratum containing the boulders. <br/><br/>
