ImplicitGeometry is a geometry representation where the shape is stored only once as a prototypical geometry. Examples are a tree or other vegetation object, a traffic light or a traffic sign. This prototypic geometry object can be re-used or referenced many times, wherever the corresponding feature occurs in the 3D city model.<br/>
